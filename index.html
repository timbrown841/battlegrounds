What is BGS?

Battle Grounds Showdown is a UK-focused online tournament series designed for both seasoned competitors and players entering their first bracket. We combine clean production, fair rules and an active Discord community.

Competitive, but welcoming

Clear rules, friendly staff and beginner brackets ensure new players can learn without being overwhelmed. Regulars enjoy a stable, high-level environment.

Tournaments & Formats

All events run through Start.gg with Discord coordination. Simple, fair formats across Tekken 8, Street Fighter 6 and Mortal Kombat 1.

Tekken 8 – Iron Fist Fridays

Double elimination, FT2 until Top 3, FT3 for finals. Random stage, winner locked to character, loser may switch.

Skill level: Open – beginners welcome.

Street Fighter 6 – Ranked Rumble

Modern or Classic controls allowed. FT2 sets, FT3 Top 3. Stable connections required, lag disputes handled by staff.

Skill level: Open – casual to competitive.

Mortal Kombat 1 – Outworld Clash

Kameos legal, exploit bans enforced. FT2 into FT3 finals. Fatal hype encouraged, disrespect optional.

Skill level: Open – newcomers encouraged.

FAQ

Common questions about Battle Grounds Showdown. For anything else, ask in #tournament-help once you join Discord.

Who can enter?

Our events are focused on players in the UK & Ireland to keep connections stable. If you are nearby in EU and have a good connection, staff may allow you at their discretion.

What do I need to play?

A copy of the relevant game, online access, a stable wired or strong Wi-Fi connection, and a Discord account to receive lobby details and report scores.

Is there an entry fee?

Standard weekly tournaments have a small entry fee to fund the prize pool and production. Some beginner or community nights may be free-to-enter.

Can beginners join?

Yes. We actively encourage new players and regularly host beginner-friendly brackets and training sessions with more experienced community members.
